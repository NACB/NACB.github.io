Urban Sprawl Division

Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a city that everyone knows. We at The NACB Food Guide know it much less well than most. But we know enough to make a few recommendations, here and there.

Sugarfish

Singapore's Banana Leaf

Sugarfish is the best sushi we have had. Admittedly we haven’t had all that much high-grade sushi. But we are confident that the fantastic flavors will hold up even to a relative expert.

Singapore’s Banana Leaf offers massive portions of excellently seasoned Singaporean food. It is truly phenomenal. And, as an additional benefit, it is located in a food court type of setup, because we value options.
