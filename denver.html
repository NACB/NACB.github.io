About Us

Welcome to scenic Claremont, California, a city so densely populated with educational institutions it has been called The City of Trees and PhDs, despite most of said educational institutions being undergraduate. Claremont is also home to some pretty damn good food, which the team here at the NACB Restaurant Guide has been more than happy to eat. So, should you find yourself in Claremont (or at one of its colleges), track down these excellent dining options:

Sanamluang

Walter's

The NACB Restaurant Guide family seeks to be the definitive guide to at least one restaurant in major metropolitan areas including (and limited to) Los Angeles, Denver, and Claremont CA. We hope to see information about high quality dining in each of these target cities shared with the masses. Or at least a handful of people.

We hope our recommendations connect you with great food if you are in one of the important cities mentioned above. If not, you are shit out of luck. We are happy to say that due to our relative insignificance, we offer no perks or incentives to our subscribers at the restaurants we recommend. Good luck!
